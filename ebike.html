Common questions

E-bike electrical FAQ

Do you work on Bosch or Carrera e-bike batteries?
No — we currently don't work on Bosch or Carrera battery systems. We work on all other e-bike electrical systems including the motor, controller, throttle, display and wiring for any brand, including Bosch and Carrera bikes — it's specifically their battery systems we can't service.
Do you work on gears or general bike mechanics?
No — we're electrical specialists, not a general bike shop. We don't touch gears, derailleurs or frame mechanics. We focus on the electrical drivetrain (battery, motor, controller, throttle, display, wiring) plus brakes and tyres.
My e-bike has lost power suddenly — what could it be?
Could be the battery, the controller, a wiring fault, or even the throttle or e-brake sensor cutting power incorrectly. We run a diagnostic to find the actual cause rather than guessing — it's £30, included in the repair cost if you go ahead.
My display won't turn on — is that expensive to fix?
Depends on the cause. Sometimes it's a loose connector or wiring fault, which is a quick and inexpensive fix. If the display unit itself has failed it needs replacing — we'll diagnose first and give you the price before doing anything.
Is a hub motor or mid-drive motor easier to repair?
Both are repairable for wiring and hall sensor faults. Mid-drive systems (Bosch, Bafang, Shimano Steps) are more integrated with the bike's electronics, so diagnostics can take a little longer, but most electrical faults are still fixable without replacing the whole unit.
Do you need an appointment?
Yes — appointment only. Call or WhatsApp to book a slot. Bring your e-bike's brand and model details if you can, it helps us prepare.
