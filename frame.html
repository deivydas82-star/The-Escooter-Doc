Common questions

Frame & folding FAQ

My handlebars feel wobbly — is that a quick fix?
Often yes. In many cases it's just a loose clamp bolt that needs tightening or reinforcing. If the stem post itself has worn or developed play, a replacement is needed. We'll inspect and tell you which it is before charging for anything.
My scooter won't fold or won't lock when folded — is that dangerous?
A folding mechanism that doesn't lock securely is a safety concern, especially if it could unfold while riding. Get it checked before continuing to use it. Most folding latch and hinge repairs are straightforward.
I cracked my deck — can it be repaired or does it need full replacement?
Depends on the severity and location of the crack. Minor cracks away from stress points can sometimes be reinforced. Significant cracks, especially near the folding mechanism or wheel mounts, usually need a full frame replacement for safety. We'll give you an honest assessment.
Is it worth replacing the frame on an older scooter?
Depends on the scooter's value and condition otherwise. If the electrics, battery and motor are all in good shape, a frame replacement can extend the scooter's life significantly for less than buying new. We'll give you a straight answer based on your specific scooter.
Can you source frame and fork parts for my specific model?
In most cases yes, for popular models. Less common or older models may take longer to source. Get in touch with your scooter make and model and we'll confirm availability and price before you commit to anything.
